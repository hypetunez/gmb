How to Submit Your Song

Follow these simple steps to submit your song to Hypetunez:

  1. Ensure your song is in MP3 format.
  2. Provide the title of your song and a brief description.
  3. Send in your cover photo/flyer of the song
  4. Click the WhatsApp button below to send your song and details directly to our team.


Kindly feel free to send feedbacks or request for songs you want on the site!
